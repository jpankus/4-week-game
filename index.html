Crystal Collector Game!

How Do You Play?

You're given a random number at the start of the game. Your goal is to click on the gems and get your total score to match this number.

Clicking on a gem will add a set amount of points to your total score.

The value of each gem is hidden from you until you click on it.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

Each time the game starts, the gems will get a new random value.
